BWINDI IMPENETRABLE PARK

Bwindi impenetrable is located in kanungu town west of uganda. it is a suitable tourist destination for vacations and tours. activities include bird watching, gorilla tracking, mountain hiking among others. Animals found there may include gorilla which are the prime view. others include birds like the parrots, finchs , among thousands of species. the town has over 10 tour lodges and hotels with 5 star accomodation and other tourist activities can be found for example garuga golf course. transport is in all forms that is air transport from kihihi airstrip, and the roads are okay.

QUEEN ELIZABETH NATIONAL PARK

Queen Elizabeth NP is the oldest and most historical in the country. it is located in kasese town west of uganda. it is a suitable tourist destination for vacations and tours, game and hunting, photograghy and many others . activities include bird watching, photgraphy,animal tracking, boat cruise on the kazinga channel among others. Animals found there may include lions, hyenas, elephants, zebras, antelopes, bongos,crocodiles. others include birds like the parrots, finchs , among thousands of species. the town has over 40 tour lodges and hotels with 5 star accomodation and other tourist activities. transport is in all forms that is air and road .

MURCHISON FALLS
